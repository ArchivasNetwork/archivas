# Earning & Rewards

Understand how RCHV rewards work on Archivas.

## Block Rewards

**Current:** 20 RCHV per block
**Frequency:** ~3,000 blocks/day
**Daily Emission:** ~60,000 RCHV/day

## Win Probability

Your chance of winning = (Your plot space / Total network space)

**Example:**
- You: 48 GB (6 k28 plots)
- Network: 55 GB total
- Your share: 87%
- Expected: 2,610 blocks/day = 52,200 RCHV/day

## Claiming Rewards

Rewards are automatic! When you win a block, 20 RCHV is sent to your farmer address immediately.

Check balance: `curl https://seed.archivas.ai/account/YOUR_ADDRESS`

## Testnet vs Mainnet

**Testnet RCHV:** No economic value, for testing only
**Mainnet RCHV:** TBD - will have market value
